HOW TO PLAY

W A S D — move / drive  ·  Mouse — look around
Shift — sprint  ·  Space — jump (handbrake in car)
E — enter / exit vehicles, stores, start missions
Right-click (hold) — aim over the shoulder  ·  Click — shoot / punch
M — city map & waypoints  ·  F — punch
1 fists  ·  2 pistol  ·  3 SMG  ·  P/Esc — pause

🗺️ Open the map with M, click anywhere (or a store/mission) — the purple route on the minimap guides you there.
🏍️ Motorcycles are fast and nimble — look for riders in traffic.
💰 Take missions at the yellow M marker.
🏪 Stores sell food, drinks, guns & armor · 🏠 houses have interiors & loot.
⭐ Crime attracts the police — lose them to drop your wanted level.
